# Mental Health Among University Students - Research Study  

This project explores the factors influencing mental health among university students using a **quantitative research approach**.  

## Research Overview  
- **Independent Variable**: Academic Pressure  
- **Dependent Variable**: Mental Health  
- **Moderator**: Teacher Support  
- **Mediator**: Sleep Quality  

## Methodology  
- Data collected from 30 university students via a survey.  
- Statistical analysis conducted using Python.
